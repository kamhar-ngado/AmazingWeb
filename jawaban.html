Diketahui:

$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f(x)}}{x} = 2$,

$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f(x)}}{{\sqrt {1 - x} - 1}}$

Ditanyakan:

tentukan $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f(x)}}{{\sqrt {1 - x} - 1}}$


Merencanakan Penyelesaian:

Substitusi nilai $f(x)$ pada $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f(x)}}{x} = 2$

Melakukan perkalian sekawan


Melaksanakan Rencana Penyelesaian:

$\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f(x)}}{x}.x = 2x$ $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} f\left( x \right) = 2x$ $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{f\left( x \right)}}{{\sqrt {1 - x} - 1}} = \mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{2x}}{{\sqrt {1 - x} - 1}}\frac{{\left( {\sqrt {1 - x} + 1} \right)}}{{\left( {\sqrt {1 - x} + 1} \right)}}$ $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{2x\left( {\sqrt {1 - x} + 1} \right)}}{{1 - x - 1}} = \mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} \frac{{2x\left( {\sqrt {1 - x} + 1} \right)}}{{ - x}}{\text{ }}$ $\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to 0} - 2\left( {\sqrt {1 - x} + 1} \right) = - 4{\text{ }}$
